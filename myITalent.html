    PXL X-factor

    The vision of PXL is based on the PXL X-factor. The X-factor consists of 4 important statements:

    • Empassion
    • Entreperneurial & Innovative
    • International collaboration
    • Multidisciplinary

    The X-factor is a way to differentiate yourself as a student from your fellow students. The I-talent course gives students an opportunity to work on those important four statements.

    I-Talent

    The I-Talent course consists of 5 different pillars:

    • Innovation
    • Inernationalization
    • Personal Development
    • Seminars
    • Student Engagement

    These pillars all have some correlation with the statements from the X-factor. In my opinion the I-talent course & portfolio is a way to show you have the X-factor. One of my own projects for I-talent is creating this personal online resume where you can also find my I-talent portfolio.
